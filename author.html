I'm Patrice Kenmoé, the creator and maintainer of AfterShift. I document labor conflicts in Sweden, with a focus on the restaurant and hospitality industry.

Based in Malmö, I investigate the legal blind spots that precarious workers face — from missing contracts to denied wages — and explore strategic and legal responses to employer misconduct.

My work is based on real cases, legal texts, and procedural analysis. AfterShift is both a personal archive and a tactical publishing project.

The underlying experience took place in winter 2024, while working as a dishwasher at Quan, a high-end restaurant in Mälmo. Despite a high workload and overall good feedback, no written contract was ever issued, and wages were paid late. The employer's classification of the departure led to a suspension of benefits....
