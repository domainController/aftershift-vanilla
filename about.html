Profil

My name is Patrice Kenmoé, I’m 47 years old. Born in Lyon, of Bamiléké ethnicity, I’ve lived in six countries. After three years in Ireland, I moved to Sweden on April 21st, 2022.

At 25, I left France for the first time: destination the Netherlands, where I worked in several call centers. Back in France, I returned to sales, then discovered the job of hotliner, my first real gateway into IT.

At 29, I started an IT training course, and before finishing it, I was recruited by HP in Barcelona, then IBM in Amsterdam. I spent two and a half years abroad, before returning to France at 32, where I worked as a consultant at Sanofi.

At 35, in conflict with my values, I walked away from everything. I left the highest-paying job I’d ever had to move to Berlin.

Between 35 and 45, I worked barely a year in total. I sometimes lived without stable housing, sometimes in emergency shelters, sometimes in rentals or with a partner. It was a time of rupture, of deep questioning, when I began to shape my own principles of life.

At 45, I moved to Sweden. I spent nearly a year in Gothenburg, then about 15 months in Stockholm, much of it in a relationship. For the past 6 months, I’ve been living in Malmö, where a new chapter has quietly started.

Since March 21 , 2025, I had begun working on the development of a dating app mvp. But on April 13, I decided to put this work on hold in order to fully dedicate myself to the research, writing, and development of Aftershift.se.
