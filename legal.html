Legal Notice

1. Purpose

This page outlines the legal and editorial stance of this investigative blog. The content published here aims to clarify facts, question power structures, and document documented abuses or irregularities involving companies or public actors. The approach is structured, evidence-based, and rooted in journalistic integrity and transparency.

2. Scope of Claims

  1. All allegations are based on verifiable documents, official records, or first-hand experiences described by the author.
  2. Documents cited or published may include court rulings, public registries, communications from institutions, and audio or visual proof when available.
  3. The aim is not to defame individuals, but to hold structures and systems accountable where wrongdoing or opacity has been detected.

3. Legal Status

  1. This site is operated independently and does not represent any political party, union, or commercial entity.
  2. It is hosted within the European Union and protected under applicable freedom of expression laws, including the European Charter of Fundamental Rights.
  3. In case of legal dispute, Swedish and EU digital rights and procedural standards apply.

4. Public Records & Transparency

  1. Many of the materials used originate from Swedish public databases (Bolagsverket, Domstolsverket, Skatteverket) or EU-accessible datasets.
  2. Fees paid for public documents are listed when relevant, to emphasize the research cost and transparency effort.
  3. Documents already made public (e.g. by legal filings) may be annotated for clarity but not redacted unless legally required.

5. Defense FAQ

  1. If you are named in an article and believe it to be in error, you have a right of reply: send a signed request to the editor via email. Include your corrections and supporting documents.
  2. No content will be removed without an adequate factual rebuttal or judicial order.
  3. Malicious threats or harassment will be publicly documented and forwarded to relevant authorities.

6. Response Rights

  1. Any party mentioned may request a formal counterstatement to be published, provided it is factual, verifiable, and respectful.
  2. Such requests will be processed within 14 days if received in writing and signed.
  3. Anonymous denials or vague disclaimers will not be considered a valid rebuttal.

7. Commenting & Feedback

  1. This blog does not currently feature a public forum or discussion board. Comments are disabled by default to preserve editorial focus and legal clarity.
  2. Readers may submit factual corrections, additional context, or feedback by contacting the author directly via email.
  3. Selected feedback may be reflected in future updates or mentioned in footnotes, at the editor’s discretion.

8. Archives & Edits

  1. All previous versions of major pages are archived for accountability.
  2. Each document may include a “last updated” timestamp with changelog upon request.
  3. Corrections and factual amendments will be timestamped and visible on the page if substantial.

9. Citation & Reuse

  1. Non-commercial reuse is allowed with credit to the author and link to the original source.
  2. Commercial reproduction, including AI scraping or integration into proprietary datasets, is prohibited without written consent.
